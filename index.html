Patient Journey

  • Entry Point: Service member accesses the Beneficiary Front Door (BFD) portal.
  • Module Selection: Chooses Behavioral Health (BH) module.
  • AI Assessment: States reason for care; Limbic AI chatbot conducts automated screening, clinical triage, and severity/urgency evaluation.
  • Smart Routing: System recommends appropriate care pathways based on results.

Key Benefits

  • Automated triage reduces wait times.
  • 24/7 availability through AI.
  • Appropriate care matching based on needs.
  • Seamless integration with military health resources.
  • Immediate crisis intervention routing when needed.
